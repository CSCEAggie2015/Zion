Zion National Park

Zion National Park was always a place that I'd learned about in school growing up in Chicago and Texas, but couldn't fathom its immense beauty and wonder from the opposite coast. After discovering my love for hiking through college in the abundantly green Texas hills, I unlocked greater proximity to a plethora of travel destinations upon moving to Las Vegas.

I couldn't believe that the Grand Canyon, Death Valley and Zion National Park were all much closer than what I was used to when driving from Dallas to San Antonio. The pandemic had just hit and my birthday was that same month in March of 2020. Instead of cancelling all my plans to party, I decided to escape to nature and finally visit Zion.
